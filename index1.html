Finding quality household services shouldn't be a guessing game.

Today's online channels for discovering service providers entail overwhelming options, coupled with disorienting review systems. Vetmi connects you to the most highly recommended service providers within your personal network and local community - taking away the hassle of finding and vetting a service thats new to you.

Our Vision

For Customers

Our mission is to streamline the process of finding quality household services by removing the need for you to "vet" potential new providers. We accomplish this by showing you recommendations from the people you trust most in your local community - your friends.

How it works:

  • For every service category, a user is allowed one "endorsement" to the provider they use and trust.
  • The providers with the most endorsements are recommended first, based on either:
    1. Endorsements from people within your social network
    2. Endorsements from people within your local community

For Service Providers

Whether you operate an established business, or are just a handy individual looking to make some money on the side, Vetmi aims to position you for success. Our local, communitiy-centric platform and unique endorsement system drive transparency around your hard work and client service reputation. As you grow and retain loyal followers via endorsements, potential new clients in the area will see your trusted notoriety.

What sets us apart?

Trusted Referrals

When looking for quality household services, who do you turn to first? Your friends.
Vetmi leverages social media to highlight which service providers your friends use and trust.

Unique Endorsement System

4 stars, 5 stars - What does that actually mean? Vetmi's unique rating system allows every user one endorsement per service category, guaranteeing each recommendation actually holds meaning.

Digital Lawn Sign

Ever wonder how the neighbors have such an incredible lawn? With Vetmi's geo-location based platform, you can identify which service provider is responsible for the work you see around your community!
